Highlights from Insights & Design Primary Research

Catalina's Insights & Design team has conducted 24 focus groups and over 40 1:1 interviews across the U.S., talking to almost 200 primary household grocery shoppers about their shopping and coupon habits. Continue on to learn more about what shoppers are saying, in their own words, about their coupon use today.

 

Grocery Shopping: Universal Truths

While we’ve talked to all types of people from all over the country, there are common grocery shopping habits and behaviors they all share.

Shoppers today are shopping at multiple stores
Varies by convenience and need

Generally, one large trip takes places weekly or monthly with fill-in trips taking place up to 2 or 3 times a week.

Shopping is seen by most as a chore.

Price is still the main worry of shoppers today.

WHO USES COUPONS, AND WHY DO THEY USE THEM?

After talking to 100s of shoppers we realized that we can’t build a “profile” or singular persona of a coupon user – shoppers today desire more control, more relevance and more value regardless of age, gender or background.
