What do you call them?

And what do you do with them?

Consumer names for Catalina Coupons

  • "Register coupons"
  • "Store coupons"
  • "Checkout coupons"
  • "Brand-sponsored coupons"
  • Catalinas - only more savvy couponers call them this, as they’ve read it on blogs
  • “I call them spy coupons. They know what I buy.”
    – Boston, July 2014

When received, the majority of consumers do read them

  • Some decide in the store to keep or throw away
  • “If it's for something I don’t buy or don’t use, I get rid of them right there.” – Boston, July 2014
  • Others put them in their bag to look at later
    • They either make their way to where coupons are kept by the shopper (a wallet, binder, drawer, etc...) or they are forgotten about
  • A few are left in the car so they "remember" better to use them… one even said she puts them "right on the dash” where she can see them
