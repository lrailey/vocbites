What came first, the list or the coupon?

The List comes first for most shoppers

They’ll start with it and then look for corresponding coupons

  • List format is still paper and pen for most
  • A common format for the “digital list” is a notes app

When does a coupon trump the list?

  • New item: “A coupon can sway me, I’m open to new ideas.”
  • Exceptionally good deals and the ability to “stack” sale items and coupons: “I will buy what’s on sale, and I also have a coupon to get double savings.”
  • Stocking up

*Consumers who make their list based primarily on coupons can be considered “extreme” couponers.
